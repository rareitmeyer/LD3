OpenSMC Service Locator Demo and Survey

This is a technology demonstration of a Service Locator for San Mateo County. We'd like to show residents local services and facilities with a map-based interface like the below. As a proof of concept, it is not intended to be pretty, or even very useful yet, but we want to use it to get feedback. Please play with the map a little, then answer the questions below. We have many questions, because we have many ideas.
